What's So Special About These 4 Buttons?

In 2012, in a small Spanish village named Jun, residents used a single online platform to discuss local matters. Everybody participated as Equals. The result is Participatory Democracy. It worked so well in Jun that Madrid and Taiwan adopted it.

UNITY is core to these successes. On a mass scale Unity can only be achieved by use of sophisticated online forum software.

Usually online groups become confused when discussing many topics simultaneously and algorithms frequently interfere with the natural flow of communications.

We use the 4 buttons at the top of this page in a specific way that focus very large community discussions, (from local to global) with little effort.
